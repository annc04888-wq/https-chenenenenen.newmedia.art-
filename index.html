 Parasitic

The term “parasitism” is often used to describe individuals or groups who rely on systems, families, or others’ resources to survive. This can be seen either as criticism or as a revelation of structural inequality.

「寄生」常被用來形容某些人或群體依附於制度、家庭或他人資源而生存。這既可能是批判，也可能是對結構性不平等的揭示。

 Parasitic

Parasitism is a survival strategy, relying on the host to obtain resources. It reminds us of the diversity and cruelty of life.

寄生是一種生存策略，依附在宿主身上獲取資源。它提醒我們生命的多樣性與殘酷性。
